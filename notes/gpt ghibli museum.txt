The Ghibli Museum in Tokyo is a must-visit destination for fans of Japanese animation and the works of Studio Ghibli. The museum features a wide range of exhibits and interactive displays that showcase the creative process behind some of the studio's most beloved films, including "My Neighbor Totoro," "Spirited Away," and "Princess Mononoke." Visitors can explore a replica of director Hayao Miyazaki's workspace, watch short films that are exclusive to the museum, and view a variety of original artwork and animation cells. The museum also houses a bookstore, gift shop, and a cafe that serves snacks and refreshments inspired by the films. As the Ghibli Museum is a popular attraction, it is recommended to book tickets in advance to ensure entry.
